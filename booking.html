What Happens Next?

Simple Steps to Finding the Right Fit

After you submit your inquiry, Ashley will review your details and determine the best next step based on your goals, timeline, and location.

Step 1

Submit Your Inquiry

Share your service interest, goals, timeline, and current challenges.

Step 2

Ashley Reviews Your Request

Your inquiry is reviewed based on service type, audience, scope, location, and preferred format.

Step 3

Receive Your Recommended Next Step

Private coaching requests may move into scheduling. Team, choir, church, school, and program requests may move into consultation, proposal, and approved dates.

Step 4

Confirm Details

Once the service path, scope, and timeline are clear, Ashley will send next steps, scheduling information, invoice, or secure payment link.
